About

A Dublin-based, four-piece rock band formed in the summer of 2020. Bringing in influences from hard rock, grunge, metal, and folk, Freak Pointing combine these elements with memorable riffs and authentic lyrics to create melodic, musically-diverse anthems for the 21st century.

From pub stages to festival fields, they deliver raw energy and tight performances that stick with you long after the last chord fades.

  • Fearghal TraynorVocals
  • Darryn DowneyLead & Rhythm Guitars
  • Daragh CosgroveBass Guitar
  • Stephen CooganDrums
